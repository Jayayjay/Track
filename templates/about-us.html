Our Story

What We Have Done

The High Voltage Track Club was founded in 2005 in Seattle by Coach Johnnie K. Williams. Athletes come from the suburbs and inner-city of Seattle alike. We are sanctioned by the National Governing body and its local Northwest Association for Track and Field.

We strive to promote excellence through Track and Field while building team, life and social skills. We offer coaching to youth ages 7–18 who are interested in improving their physical fitness, mental toughness, and teamwork through organized competition.

We also strive to send each and every one of our athletes on their way with a memorable experience — one that shapes them beyond the finish line.

About The Club

Train. Compete. Succeed.

Founded in 2005 by Coach Johnnie K. Williams, High Voltage Track Club gives Seattle youth a world-class training environment. We develop athletes from the ground up — physically, mentally, and as community members.

Sanctioned by USATF and the Northwest Association, our athletes compete locally and nationally, earning medals, scholarships, and lifelong skills.
